Banitsa

Description

Banitsa, also transliterated as banica and banitza is a traditional pastry dish from Bulgaria. It is typically eaten for breakfast and can be served hot or cold. Banitsa is prepared by layering a mixture of whisked eggs, plain yogurt, and pieces of white brined cheese between filo pastry and then baking it in an oven.

If you have ever eaten Banitsa than I don't need to tell you how good it is. If you haven't than you are in the right place! This authentic Bulgarian dish will surely become one of your new favorites!
